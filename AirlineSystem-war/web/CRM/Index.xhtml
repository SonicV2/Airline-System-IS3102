Analytical CRM System

Recency Score is calculated on how many days since the customer last flown with Merlion Airlines compared to the current date.



Frequency Score is the amount of times the customer flew with Merlion Airlines in the last year.



Monetary Score is the amount the customer spent with Merlion Airlines in the last year.



Customer Lifetime Value is a prediction of how valuable a customer is to Merlion Airlines.
